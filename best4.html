Responsible Gaming

Most people play for fun and entertainment. However, some people see gambling as a way to make money, spend more than they can afford, or use gambling to escape daily problems. The following tips can help you play safely:

  1. Do not think of gambling as a way to make money.
  2. Only gamble with money you can afford to lose.
  3. Set a money limit in advance.
  4. Set a time limit in advance.
  5. Never chase your losses.
